About Me

Mother of humans and a cat. Passionate about VR/AR, I always put myself in situations where I have to brainstorm, solve problems and bring this amazing technology in a creative and accessible way for businesses and people.

I have participated in a few competitions, the most recent one was the Oculus VR hackathon, where my team won and was given Oculus Go and also invited to San Jose, California for the F8 Hackathon and Conference.

I’m currently co-leading the Facebook Developer Circle: Toronto, my goal is to encourage women into tech and bring them to the community to help, teach, learn and support each other
